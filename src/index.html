OfficerCoin tokens

The OfficerCoin (OFC) is CryptoPolice's token that is essential to the function of platform. OFC is an access token that enables CryptoPolice platform services. Tokens also will be used to reward platform users for reporting and verifying information. OFC will also provide additional motivation for users who will provide quality information and verify it.

Aim of CryptoPolice

Aim of CryptoPolice is to become a leader in efficient resistance to scam by providing society with state-of-the-art solutions in the sphere of safe operations in the market of cryptocurrencies.

Scam table

CryptoPolice provides a list of all objects verified and defined by CryptoPolice as a scam.

Project table

Knowledge base

Multilevel verification

Roadmap

EMERGENCE OF THE IDEA
1st quarter
2017

Sharp increase of cryptocurrency popularity and mass proliferation of scam. Analysis of existing methods of protection from scam impact.

Emergence of the idea of CryptoPolice.

CONCEPT ELABORATION
2nd quarter
2017

Detailed research of scam, its types and threats that it makes for the society. Emergence of an idea of multi-level verification algorithm, system of rewards and a user’s role “Officer”.

Elaboration of a concept of independent domestic economics of CryptoPolice.

WhitePaper elaboration.

DEVELOPEMENT
3rd quarter
2017

Further development of the idea for public presentation.

Development of a landing page, video footage, smart contract. Elaboration of a marketing strategy and processing of advertising.

Start of CryptoPolice Academy development - first part of CryptoPolice platform to educate officers.

PUBLISHING
4th quarter
2017

Publishing idea to crypto community. Get first feedback's from community. Start collaboration with partners and advisors.

Accomplishment of the preparation of crowdfunding:
• creation of the support system;
• smart contract testing and auditing;
• work with partners and the society (marketing, video presentations, arranging webinars, participation in conferences).
• creation of the automatic system of referral bonus distribution.
• “Early bird” system issue - the system of registration of first Officers.
• launch of the advertising campaign.
• deploying a smart contract.

Development of CryptoPolice academy - education of officers is the foundation of efficient and accurate CryptoPolice platform.

CROWDFUNDING AND PLATFORM DEVELOPMENT
1st quarter
2018

Launch crowdfunding.

Proceed token distribution.

Launching on exchanges.

Collecting feedback and recommendations from the community.

Continue CryptoPolice platform development - a user’s personal office and CryptoPolice wallet; update of the system of Officers’ training and certification;

First alpha version for partners and “Early bird” Officers.
A repeated collection of the community feedback and recommendations

First public demonstration to the whole community.

CONTINUATION OF THE PLATFORM DEVELOPMENT
2nd - 3rd quarter
2018

The launch of the system updates of CryptoPolice Academy
Development of the CryptoPolice storage system on the Ethereum blockchain.
Development of mechanisms of data submission and verification - scam, crypto projects, publications (interactions with smart contracts).
Development of the escrow contract on Ethereum.

Strategies for the platform promotion.

Second closed demo. Further collection of feedback and recommendations from the community.

Second public demo to the whole community.

Development of the system of raising investment with the use of the escrow contract. Development of WatchDog system - plugin for Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Opera, Safary. Development of a mobile WatchDog version (IOS, Android) and bots for social networks (Facebook, Twitter) and messengers (Whatssapp, Telegram).
Closed testing and collection of feedback and recommendations.

Public demo testing and collection of feedback and recommendations.

Stabilization of the developed functionality and launch processing.
Preparation of advertising channels for launch.

Preparation of Officers for launch.

LAUNCH OF THE FIRST VERSION OF CRYPTOPOLICE PLATFORM
4rd quarter
2018

Closed launch of the first version of CryptoPolice platform.
Repeated collection of feedback and recommendations.

Public launch of the first version of CryptoPolice platform.
Repeated collection of feedback and recommendations.

Launch of the marketing campaign.
Continuation of the platform development and update.

UPDATE AND SUPPORT
1st and further
2019 and further

Support, updates and improvements of the platform.
Development of API system.
Development of the partners’ network and collaboration with cryptocurrency exchanges.
Development of the rating systems for users and Officers.

Launch of the second version - stable version of CryptoPolice.

Continue with support, updates and improvement of the platform.
