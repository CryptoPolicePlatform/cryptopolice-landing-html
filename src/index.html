What is the CryptoPolice platform?

CryptoPolice is first community based cryptocurrency police platform and blockchain technology.

Main goal is to protect and inform investors from potential risks on investment process. Also provide legit project and information database for cryptocurrency world users

Officers tokens

The officers token (OFC) is essential to the functioning of the CryptoPolice platform. Tokens will be used to reward platform users for reporting and verifying information. This is needed to provide additional motivation for user who will provide quality information and do fastest verifications.
